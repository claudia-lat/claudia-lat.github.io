I am a Bioengineer with a PhD in Cognitive Robotics, Interaction and Rehabilitation Technologies. I current work in the Artificial and Mechanical Intelligence (AMI) at Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia, Genoa, Italy. My scientific interests include human modeling, biomechanics and human dynamics estimation for physical human-robot interaction. My non-scientific interests include cooking (spare-time blogger at Pomodori al Sole), reading and photography.
